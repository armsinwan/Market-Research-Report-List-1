<p><p>The wind turbine couplings market comprises various types designed to enhance drivetrain performance and reliability. Composite-disc couplings utilize lightweight materials to reduce weight while providing flexibility and damping, enhancing efficiency and reducing wear. Normal driveline couplings, which include rigid and flexible types, connect different components of the turbine, ensuring optimal power transmission and accommodating misalignment. Both types play crucial roles in minimizing maintenance needs, improving operational efficiency, and extending the overall lifespan of wind turbine systems.</p></p>
