Wave type clutches utilize a flexible design to accommodate varying loads, enhancing efficiency during cycles. Drum type clutches focus on direct connection and smooth motion, ideal for heavy-duty machines. Stirring type clutches promote uniform agitation by integrating stirring mechanisms, ensuring optimal washing performance. Each type caters to different washing machine designs, offering unique advantages in terms of efficiency, durability, and operational effectiveness.</p></p>
