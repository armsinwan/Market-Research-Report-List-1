<p><p>The Smart Remote Control market is primarily divided into two types: IR (Infrared) Smart Remote Controls and Radio Smart Remote Controls. IR Smart Remote Controls operate by emitting infrared signals to communicate with devices such as TVs and audio systems, offering a simple and direct control method. In contrast, Radio Smart Remote Controls utilize radio frequency signals, enabling a broader range of operation and the ability to control multiple devices from greater distances, enhancing user convenience and functionality.</p></p>
