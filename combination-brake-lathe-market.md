<p><p>The Combination Brake Lathe market is divided into two main types: On-Car Brake Lathe Machines and Off-Car Brake Lathe Machines. On-Car Brake Lathes are designed to machine brake rotors while they are still mounted on the vehicle, allowing for quick and efficient servicing without the need for removal. Off-Car Brake Lathes, on the other hand, require the brake components to be removed from the vehicle for precision machining. Both types enhance braking performance, but their applications cater to different workshop needs.</p></p>
