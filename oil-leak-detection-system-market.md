<p><ul><li>Passive Leak Detection Systems</li><li>Active Leak Detection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oil Leak Detection System market is classified into two main types: passive and active leak detection systems. Passive leak detection systems rely on materials or devices that naturally detect leaks without intervention, often using absorbent materials or visual indicators. In contrast, active leak detection systems utilize sensors and monitoring technologies that continuously assess pressure, flow rates, and other parameters to identify leaks in real-time. Both systems are crucial for minimizing environmental impact and ensuring the safety of oil and gas operations.</p></p>
