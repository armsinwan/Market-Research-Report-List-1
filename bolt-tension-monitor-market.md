<p><strong>The Bolt Tension Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Testing</li><li>Sensor Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bolt Tension Monitor market consists of two primary types: Ultrasonic Testing and Sensor Testing. Ultrasonic Testing utilizes high-frequency sound waves to evaluate the tension in bolts, offering a non-destructive method to ensure structural integrity. Sensor Testing employs various sensors to measure bolt tension directly, providing real-time data for monitoring and maintenance. Both methods are crucial in industries such as construction, aerospace, and automotive, where precise tension measurements are essential for safety and reliability in bolted joints.</p></p>
