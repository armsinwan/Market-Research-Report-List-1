<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Powered</li><li>Pneumatic Powered</li><li>Electric Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart parking bollards are categorized into three main types based on their power sources: hydraulic, pneumatic, and electric. Hydraulic-powered bollards utilize fluid pressure to rise and lower, providing robust security and high resistance to impact. Pneumatic-powered bollards operate on compressed air, offering quick deployment and retraction with less energy consumption. Electric-powered bollards use an electric motor for operation, providing precise control and integration with smart systems. Each type enhances urban mobility and space management, catering to different operational needs.</p></p>
