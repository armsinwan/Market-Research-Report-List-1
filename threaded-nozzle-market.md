<p><strong>The Threaded Nozzle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Thread Nozzle</li><li>External Thread Nozzle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The threaded nozzle market is classified into two main types: internal thread nozzles and external thread nozzles. Internal thread nozzles feature threads on the inside, allowing them to fit snugly onto corresponding male components, providing a secure connection for fluid or gas flow. In contrast, external thread nozzles possess threads on the outside, enabling them to be screwed into female fittings. Both types are essential in various industrial applications, such as fluid transfer, spray systems, and manufacturing processes, ensuring efficient performance and reliability.</p></p>
