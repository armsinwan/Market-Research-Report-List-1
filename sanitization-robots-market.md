<p><p>Sanitization robots are categorized primarily into two types: UV-C sanitization robots and HPV (Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor) sanitization robots. UV-C sanitization robots utilize ultraviolet light to eliminate pathogens on surfaces, effectively disinfecting spaces without the use of chemicals. In contrast, HPV sanitization robots deploy hydrogen peroxide vapor to penetrate surfaces and kill bacteria and viruses, ensuring thorough disinfection. Both types are increasingly essential in healthcare, hospitality, and public spaces, enhancing hygiene and reducing the spread of infectious diseases.</p></p>
