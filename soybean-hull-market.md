<p><p>The soybean hull market primarily consists of two forms: loose form and pellet form. Loose form soybean hulls are unprocessed and available in bulk, offering flexibility for various applications like animal feed or organic matter in soil improvement. In contrast, pellet form soybean hulls are processed into compact pellets, making them easier to handle and transport. This form enhances nutrient density and digestibility, catering to specific dietary needs in livestock and providing convenience for feed manufacturers.</p></p>
