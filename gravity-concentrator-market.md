<p><p>The gravity concentrator market can be divided into two main types: continuous and intermittent. Continuous gravity concentrators operate non-stop, efficiently processing materials in a steady flow, making them ideal for large-scale operations aiming for consistent output. On the other hand, intermittent gravity concentrators function in batch mode, processing materials in defined periods, suitable for smaller operations or when varying feed conditions are present. Each type serves different operational needs in mineral processing, optimizing recovery rates based on specific requirements.</p></p>
