<p><p>The Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (ELCB) market is categorized into Voltage Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (VELCB) and Current Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (CELCB). VELCBs detect earth faults by monitoring voltage imbalances, automatically disconnecting the circuit to prevent hazards. In contrast, CELCBs measure the current flow and trigger disconnection when detecting leakage currents exceeding safe limits. These devices enhance electrical safety by protecting against electric shocks and preventing fire risks, catering to residential, commercial, and industrial applications.</p></p>
